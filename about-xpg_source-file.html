About XPG

XPG History

The XPG Play Hub was initially designed as a school project in Jamaica by
a team of first year students who called themselves: ‘XPG Syndicates.’
Hence, the name of website. Inspired to make gaming much more attractive
whilst cost effective, XPG set out to create a platform for
gamers from all around the world to come together and play
their favorite games online.

The intent of XPG, is to make premiered (just released) or newest games more attainable to gamers once
uploaded to our platform; readily available for renting to provide hands-on experience to players yearning
to try out these new games, but limited to hardware specifications due to the demand graphics
and texture of these new games.

XPG continue to build out creative and innovative strategies to provide an interesting and exciting user
interface for all our gamers. In 2023, the team implemented additional encryption layers in the backend,
as XPG strongly promote data security. And as such, we will not tolerate hackers, which is where our policy
is designed via an embedding hacking control system. The XPG team remains resolute to make our
platform safe and secure when it comes to payment integrity for utmost assurance while you game
and find friends.
